Sell your game worldwide

We handle international taxes and rules so you can easily sell your game worldwide. With only one line of code, turn your website into an elegant global e-commerce platform.

Playable Demos

Using the power of asm.js, customers can play your demo directly on your website prior to purchasing.

Steam Key Fulfillment

If your game is being distributed via Steam or another platform, we'll distribute keys to both new purchasers and Kickstarter backers.

Limited-time discounts

Run exclusive limited-time discounts directly on your website. You choose the scheduling by using the Humble Dashboard.

Payment Processing

The Humble Widget handles all payment processing for credit cards and major international payment providers, and you benefit from our discounts on payment processor fees.

Digital Content Fulfillment

We deliver the game to your customers and cover all bandwidth costs. The customer has the convenience of storing their downloads in their Humble Library.

Real Time Analytics

See your sales in real time on your Humble Dashboard.

Customer Support

Our customer service team handles payment processing and fulfillment questions. (But it's up to you to handle the questions about getting past level seven or defeating the giant octodragonsaur!)

Fraud Prevention

You benefit from our years of experience fighting fraud. No need to build complex anti-fraud mechanisms to keep the botnets at bay -- we're all too familiar with them.

Gifting

Your customers can easily buy gifts for friends. Friends may add the game to their Humble Library, unlock it on Steam, or download it directly to their PC.
