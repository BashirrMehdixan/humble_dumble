    raised for charity and counting...

    Since Humble's first bundle in 2010, raising money for charity has been at our core. When you buy one of our bundles, or games from our Store, or a Humble Choice membership, you're also helping people and causes in need.
    Thanks to our community's spirit and generosity, to date we've raised over US$253 million for charity. Together, we continue to be a force for good in the world.

    OUR FOCUS

    To make the greatest impact we can, we join forces with charities whose work is connected to our five key focus areas. Together, we can take on the most important issues touching the lives of communities around the globe.

    Learn more in our latest video and social impact report.

    In 2020, we established the Black Game Developer Fund, which contributes resources, guidance, and other support to Black game developers, who have long lacked the type of access fundamental to having a level playing field in the industry. Learn more at Blackgamedevfund.com.

    HUMBLERS FOR GOOD

    We're a small team, but our shared desire to be a force for good drives everything we do inside the company as well, with charitable giving being a key part of our reward and recognition programs and other company activities.

    These include donation matching, time off for community service, a work-anniversary giving program, and even surprise goodies and events that support small businesses who share our values. We believe every action, big or small, adds up to make a difference in the world.
